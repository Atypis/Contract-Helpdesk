This Cannot Yet Be a Swap

Both contracts are pre-contracts. A "Conditional Swap Reservation" is possible.

What This Means

In this case, we allow the customer to conclude a second VV which functions as a "Conditional Swap Reservation". This means: if and once the second VV is converted into an actual KV nach VV and fully closed (property purchase completed, contract converted, payment made ‚Äî everything), the customer gets the right to withdraw from / cancel their first contract.

?

Why this structure? MYNE wants to enable swaps, but not to our economic detriment. If we allowed an immediate swap and the second VV doesn't go through (e.g., not enough interest, property falls through, due diligence issues), MYNE would lose the first contract without recourse. The conditional logic protects against this risk.

Required: Add Special Condition to Second VV

The second Vorvertrag must include a special clause that grants the customer an extended withdrawal right for the first VV. See details below.

Clause Must Include:

  • First VV receives an extended right of withdrawal IF the second VV is converted into a KV nach VV and fully closed
  • If the second VV is cancelled or doesn't proceed, the first VV remains completely valid and binding

Important: Second Downpayment Required

Customer must make a downpayment for the second VV even though one was already made for the first. The first downpayment will be returned when the second contract is fully closed and the first is withdrawn.

What This Means

?

Why this structure? MYNE won't buy back the first share until the second VV converts to a full KV and is completely closed. This protects MYNE from repurchasing a share when the new property might not go through (e.g., not enough interest, due diligence issues, customer payment default). Only when everything is secured does the swap-away right activate.

Required: Add Conditional Swap-Away Right to New VV

The new Vorvertrag must include a clause granting:

Condition Details:

  • Extended swap-away right for the first contract (KV)
  • Activates when the second VV is converted to KV nach VV and fully closed (including payment)
  • Upon activation: MYNE repurchases the first share and the swap is completed
